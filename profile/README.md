## What is TaskPaper for Mac?

TaskPaper for Mac is a **minimalist and powerful plain-text task manager** designed to help you organize your projects and todos with remarkable simplicity and efficiency. Built specifically for macOS, it uses a clean, text-based format that is both human-readable and incredibly powerful.

The core of TaskPaper is its unique syntax where you define projects with `@` tags and tasks with `-` bullets. This approach provides unparalleled flexibility, allowing you to create a system that perfectly fits your personal workflow or professional project management needs.

With **TaskPaper Mac**, you can quickly capture ideas, structure complex projects with nested tasks, and use tags for context, priority, and due dates. The application features a powerful search and filter bar to instantly focus on specific tasks or projects. Its clean, distraction-free interface ensures you stay focused on your work, not on managing your task manager.

Fully optimized for **macOS Ventura and Sonoma**, TaskPaper delivers smooth performance on all Apple hardware, from MacBook Air to Mac Studio. It integrates seamlessly with the Mac ecosystem, supporting Dark Mode, keyboard shortcuts, and AppleScript for automation.

Whether you're a developer managing a sprint, a writer organizing research, or a student planning assignments, TaskPaper provides a future-proof and versatile system for getting things done.
